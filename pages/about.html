About us!

History

Sometime in the 1970's, A swiss Italian called "Francesco Codoni" immigratedd to Scotland in the 1790's. Throughout the 19th century, the family toured Scotland entertaining towns and cities. In the 1860's, The Codonas bought steam powered fairground rides for touring the country.

Alfred and Gordon took over the Codona's Pleasure Fairs business from their father, which they ran until 1960. Alfred took over the beachfront amusement park which is now known as Codona's today.
